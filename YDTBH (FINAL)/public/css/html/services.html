Services









Why Choose Us

Your Health is Our Priority

Counselling and Psychotherapy

You Deserve To Be Happy provides individual and group counselling and psychotherapy services where you can talk about and work through your emotional difficulties with a trained counsellor/therapist. This is safe and non-judgmental environment for anyone working on their mental health or even just looking for personal growth.

Workplace Wellbeing program

You Deserve To Be Happy provides EAP programs that help you understand the mental and emotional needs of your employees and give them counselling services that creates psychological safety at work.

Support Groups

You Deserve To Be Happy facilitates support groups on issues that have a shared experience, creating a safe environment for likeminded individuals to share, learn and grow together.

Outreach Programs

You Deserve To Be Happy outreach programs are centered around creating awareness regarding mental health. The sessions reach out to all sections of the community, helping educate them on their own mental health and taking proactive steps to care for it.

HelpLine

You Deserve To Be Happy provides a free-of-cost telephone helpline at +591 77978307 wherein anyone across the country can call and have a conversation about any aspect of their mental health. It is a safe, confidential and non-judgmental space to provide psychological first aid.
